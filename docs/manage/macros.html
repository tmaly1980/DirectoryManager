Directory Manager 2.0 Documentation: Macros

Macros are special pieces of text in the form of '${something}' that represent a certain value to be calculated upon saving an entry.

For example, you can specify via '${uidNum}' for the uidNumber of a UNIX account that the uidNumber should be calculated to be the next available uidNumber in the directory, or use '${company_domain}' to help determine a user's email address based upon their employer/company.

Macros are specific per module (i.e. users, groups, etc.), and are specifically documented in the respective Managing Specific Modules section later in this documentation.
