Directory Manager 2.0 Documentation: Entry Tracking

When viewing or editing an entry (as a logged-in user), you can see a section at the bottom called 'Internal Tracking'. This documents who created the entry and when, as well as who last modified the entry and when.

* NOTE: When performing modifications on an entry, the page must be refreshed to show updated modification information.
