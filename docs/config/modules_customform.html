Directory Manager 2.0 Documentation: Customizable Form Drop-Downs

The data entry forms for adding and editing entries can be customized for each enabled module. It can take plain-text fields and change them into a pre-defined list of option items for easier form fill-out. There will ALWAYS be the option of entering in an 'other' value (i.e. one not in the predefined list). WARNING: Colon characters (':') are NOT allowed.
To ADD or EDIT a drop-down list for a particular entry field:
  1. Find the exact LDAP schema attribute name, available via the HTML source code of the form in question.
  2. If it already is in the left-side list ('Form Field'), click on it.
  3. If it is not already in the list, click on 'Add' and then enter in the name. Click on it to then highlight it.

  4. Start adding or removing possible values from the right-hand list ('Field Values'), by typing clicking on 'Add' and entering in values, or clicking on a value and then 'Remove'.
  5. Once you are done editing the list of a specific form field, click on 'Save' in between the two lists.
  6. Once you click on 'Update' at the bottom of the screen, the settings will be saved and will take effect immediately.
To REMOVE certain options from a drop-down list:
  1. Click on the field name in the 'Form Field' list on the left.
  2. Click on the item on the right-hand list ('Form Values') and click on 'Remove'.
  3. Click on 'Save' in between the lists to make the list up-to-date.
  4. Click on 'Update' at the bottom of the screen for it to take effect in the configuration file.
To REMOVE an entire drop-down list (and have the form option revert back to just a plain-text field):
  1. Click on the field name in the 'Form Field' left-hand list.
  2. Click on 'Remove' from the left-hand side, underneath the left-hand list.
  3. Click on 'Update' at the bottom of the screen to save the configuration.
